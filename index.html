Football Guide

Learn how to play every role on the football field

Goalkeeper

Positioning

Stay alert and position yourself between the ball and the goal. Anticipate shots and adjust your stance accordingly.

Shot Stopping

React quickly to block or catch shots. Use your hands, feet, and body to cover as much of the goal as possible.

Distribution

After gaining possession, distribute the ball accurately to teammates to start an attack.

Defender

Marking

Stay close to opposing attackers and prevent them from receiving or shooting the ball.

Tackling

Time your tackles to win the ball cleanly without committing fouls.

Positioning

Maintain a strong defensive line and cover spaces left by teammates.

Midfielder

Passing

Distribute the ball efficiently to teammates and control the tempo of the game.

Support

Assist both defense and attack, providing options for passes and covering spaces.

Vision

Read the game and anticipate plays to create opportunities.

Forward

Finishing

Convert chances into goals with accurate shooting and composure.

Movement

Make intelligent runs to get into scoring positions and create space for teammates.

Pressing

Apply pressure on defenders to force mistakes and regain possession.
